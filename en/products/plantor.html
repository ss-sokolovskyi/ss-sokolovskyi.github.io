What is a Hemostatic Agent?

A Hemostatic Agent is a life-saving substance designed for rapid blood clotting in emergency situations.

Our 100% organic hemostatic agent is specifically developed for quick and effective control of severe bleeding, making it an essential tool in both medical facilities and first aid scenarios.

How does it work?

Our hemostatic agent accelerates the natural blood clotting process. When applied to a wound, it helps form a stable clot quickly, significantly reducing blood loss and the risk of complications. This is especially important in emergency situations where time is critical.

Areas of Use:

– Emergency Response: Perfect for paramedics, EMTs, first responders, and other rapid response personnel;

– Military Use: Essential for field medics and military personnel in combat situations;

– First Aid Kits: A vital addition to personal and professional first aid kits;

– Surgical Use: Improves hemostasis during operations and medical procedures;

– Veterinary Use: Effective for controlling bleeding in animals during emergencies.

Key Features:

– 100% Organic: Made from natural materials, ensuring safety and compatibility with the body;

– Speed: Promotes blood clotting in seconds, minimizing blood loss;

– Easy to Use: Suitable for both professionals and untrained individuals;

– Universal: Effective for a wide range of wounds, including cuts, punctures, and lacerations;

– Portable: Lightweight and compact, perfect for emergency kits on the go.

Safety and Effectiveness:

Our hemostatic agent has undergone all necessary testing and registration procedures, as evidenced by the relevant documentation. It is safe for both humans and animals, with minimal risk of side effects.

Always follow the included instructions.

How to Use?

1. Clean the wound: If possible, clean the wound area to remove any contamination;

2. Apply the agent: Open the package and apply the hemostatic agent directly to the wound;

3. Apply pressure: Press on the wound to help the agent form a clot;

4. Seek medical help: In case of serious wounds, seek professional medical help immediately after applying the agent.
