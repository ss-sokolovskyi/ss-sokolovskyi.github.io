Ivan Ivanovych Sokolovskyi

1939-2015

Open biography in PDF

1939

Ivan Ivanovych Sokolovskyi was born on August 24, 1939, in the village of Novomikhaylivka, Zaporizhzhia region.

1957

In 1957, he graduated from School No. 71 in Zaporizhzhia with a Gold Medal and was admitted to the Radiophysics Faculty of Kharkiv University without exams.

1957-1962

In 1962, he graduated from Kharkiv University and was assigned to work in the defense industry at the Dnipropetrovsk Machine-Building Plant as one of the top graduates.

1968-2000

In 1968, he was invited to work at the Ukrainian Academy of Sciences, at the Dnipropetrovsk Branch of the Institute of Technical Mechanics, which later became the leading institute of the rocket and space industry of Ukraine. За період праці з 1968 до 2000 року отримав десятки винагород за успішне виконання планів НДР та впровадження у виробництво; за авторські свідоцтва; почесні грамоти, у тому числі впровадження винаходів із великим економічним ефектом.

1974

In 1974, he successfully defended his dissertation on the topic "Research of Mechanisms of Wideband Operation of Gunn Diodes in the Mode of Domain Suppression" to obtain the degree of Candidate of Physical and Mathematical Sciences.

1986

In 1986, he was awarded the honorary badge "Inventor of the USSR".

1998

In 1998, on the occasion of the 80th anniversary of the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine, Ivan Ivanovych was awarded a jubilee medal for his significant contribution to the scientific research of the institute.

2000-2015

In 2000, he was transferred to the newly established Institute of Transport Systems and Technologies of the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine "Transmag", where he worked as a leading researcher until 2015.

2004

In 2004, he was awarded the title of "Best Inventor of Ukraine" by the Presidium of the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine (Resolution No. 139 dated July 2, 2004, signed by the President of NAS of Ukraine, Academician B. Ye. Paton).

2007, 2008, 2014

He was awarded diplomas of the winner of the All-Ukrainian competition "Invention 2007", "Invention 2008", "Invention 2014" in the nomination "Best Invention in the Field of Medicine and Pharmacology" in 2007-2008 and 2014.

2008

In 2008, by the decision of the editorial board of the International Biographical Directory "Who's Who in Science and Engineering" (USA), Ivan Ivanovych Sokolovskyi was included in the 10th issue of the publication (VIP code EM10D). Thanks to the breadth of his scientific interests, his name became known far beyond the borders of our country.

2009

In 2009, for his discovery in the field of biology and biophysics, he was awarded the Honorary Medal of the Russian Academy of Natural Sciences "To the Author of Scientific Discovery" named after Nobel Prize laureate Pyotr Leonidovych Kapitsa.

2013

On May 16, 2013, for outstanding merits in inventive activity, Ivan Ivanovych Sokolovskyi was awarded the honorary title "Merited Inventor of Ukraine" by the Decree of the President of Ukraine No. 279/2013 dated May 16, 2013.

1939-2015

Over 53 years of his professional career, Ivan Ivanovych published more than 250 scientific works, 2 monographs, over 100 patents for inventions, about 200 inventions, and 2 discoveries. When asked where he gets his information from, he would answer: "from Space." Perhaps it was a joke, but perhaps it was true: with geniuses, it's probably just like that.

Ivan Ivanovych Sokolovskyi passed away on March 7, 2015, at the age of 75. His life can and is an example for young generations entering life and seeking proper role models.
