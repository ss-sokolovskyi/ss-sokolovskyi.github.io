Serhii Ivanovych Sokolovskyi

Director of International Nobel Information Center. Official Representation in Ukraine

Vice-Rector, Professor of the Department of Fundamental Disciplines of European Medical University

Member (Academician) of the Academy of Technological Sciences of Ukraine, Academy of Political and Legal Sciences of Ukraine. Honorary Professor of the Academy of Holistic Medicine (Falkensee, Germany). Advisor to the Director of the National Coordination Center for Anti-Corruption. Chairman of the Honor Council of the World Volunteers Charity Foundation, Director of the Enkurs Charity Foundation.

1964

Serhii Ivanovych Sokolovskyi was born on April 16, 1964, in Dnipropetrovsk.

1982-1987

In 1987, he graduated from the Dnipropetrovsk Medical Institute and received a higher education in the specialty "Stomatology". He was already awarded the badge "Inventor of the USSR" during his studies.

1987-2004

After graduating from the institute, he worked as a specialist in medical institutions in Dnipropetrovsk, combining practical activities with teaching at the Dnipropetrovsk Medical Institute. From 1992, he worked as the head of the department at the regional clinical hospital named after I.I. Mechnikov.

2003

In parallel with medical activities, he studied at the Dnipropetrovsk Regional Institute of Public Administration of the National Academy of Public Administration under the President of Ukraine, which he graduated in 2003 and received a master's degree in public administration.

In the same year, he received a master's degree in humanities from London Metropolitan University (London Metropolitan University, London, UK).

Also in 2003, he defended his dissertation at the Odessa Institute of Stomatology of the Academy of Medical Sciences of Ukraine, where he was awarded the academic title of Candidate of Medical Sciences.

2004-...

Since 2004, he has held the position of Vice-Rector for International Relations at the European Medical University, and since 2008, he has been the Director of the International Research and Educational Center for Traditional Medicine of this University.

2005-2020

In 2005, he was invited to the Dnipropetrovsk Regional Institute of Public Administration of the National Academy of Public Administration under the President of Ukraine as a senior researcher, where after completing his postgraduate studies, from 2008 to 2020, he worked as an associate professor of the department of public administration and local self-government.

2007

In 2007, he graduated from the Dnipropetrovsk National University named after O. Gonchar, Faculty of Advanced Training in the specialty of Medical Psychology, where after graduation, he worked as an associate professor of the department of medical psychology, psychodiagnostics, and psychotherapy on a part-time basis.

2007-2019

Two years in a row in 2007-2008, and in 2014, together with co-authors, he was awarded the Diploma of the winner of the All-Ukrainian contest "Invention 2007", "Invention 2008", "Invention 2014" in the nomination "The best invention in the field of medicine and pharmacology".

He was awarded the Order "Scientist of the Year 2019", the Honorary Certificate of the Ukrainian Federation of Scientists of Ukraine, the Gratitude of the Ministry of Health of Ukraine, and the Certificate of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine. For his scientific discovery in the field of biophysics, he was awarded the honorary medal named after P.L. Kapitsa.

He was included in the book "Scientific Elite of Ukraine". He was included in the fifth edition of "Flagships of Modern Medicine" of the best workers in the medical industry of Ukraine. He is the author of over 200 scientific and educational works, monographs, patents for inventions, and co-author of a scientific discovery in the field of biophysics.
