Open biography in PDF

1994

Sokolovskyi Serhii Serhiiovych was born on December 14, 1994, in Dnipropetrovsk.

2012-2018

In 2018, he graduated from Dniprovskyi Medical Institute of Traditional and Alternative Medicine and received higher education in General Medicine (Diploma with honors C18 №031582), qualification - Doctor.

2018-2020

From 2018 to 2020, he underwent internship at the Department of Physical Rehabilitation, Sports Medicine and Valology of Dnipropetrovsk Medical Academy of the Ministry of Health of Ukraine and received professional qualification "Sports Medicine Doctor" in the specialty "Sports Medicine" (Physician Specialist Certificate №11274).

He underwent practical training at the Dnipropetrovsk Medical and Physical Culture Dispensary as a sports medicine intern physician.

2019

In 2019, he graduated from the Dnipropetrovsk Regional Institute of State Administration of the National Academy of State Administration under the President of Ukraine and received higher education in Public Administration and Administration (Diploma with honors M19 №121679), qualification - Master of Public Administration and Administration.

He underwent internship at the Department of Health of Dnipropetrovsk Regional State Administration.

2020

In 2020, he underwent advanced training at the Dnipropetrovsk Medical Academy of the Ministry of Health of Ukraine and received professional qualification "Physical and Rehabilitation Medicine Doctor" in the specialty "Physical and Rehabilitation Medicine" (Physician Specialist Certificate №57/20).

He worked as a physician in the medical department of the branch "Pension 'Sokil'" of DP "KB 'Pivdenne'" named after M.K. Yangel.

2020-2024

In 2024, he completed his doctoral studies at the National Technical University "Dnipro Polytechnic" and received the degree of Doctor of Philosophy (PhD diploma H24 №004012), field of knowledge "Public Administration and Administration". Thesis topic: "Improvement of the Medical Rehabilitation System in Ukraine: Public Administration Aspect".

Author of 8 scientific articles, theses, and two inventions.

Author of the international joint educational program of the European Medical University (Ukraine) and the University of Klaipeda (Lithuania).
